Playing Conditions

01. Match Format

Matches follow a strict T20 format. Powerplay persists for the first 6 overs. Standard ICC regulations apply unless specified otherwise by the tournament committee.

02. The Ball

Official tournament balls (Grade A Leather) will be provided for every inning. Replacements only occur if the ball is deemed unfit by both umpires.

03. Player Conduct

A zero-tolerance policy exists for dissent. Any breach of the spirit of cricket results in immediate point deduction or disqualification.

04. Rain & Stoppage

Duckworth-Lewis-Stern (DLS) method will be utilized for all rain-affected matches. A minimum of 5 overs must be completed for a valid result.

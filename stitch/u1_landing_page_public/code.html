Standardized Play

Tournament Rules

01

Official T20 Standard

Matches will strictly follow the international T20 format with 20 overs per side. All equipment must be pre-approved by the tournament committee.

02

Player Eligibility

Each team must register exactly 15 players. All players must present valid identification at check-in. No changes are permitted after the first ball is bowled.

03

Umpire Finality

The umpire's decision is final in all circumstances. Any display of dissent or poor sportsmanship will result in immediate penalty points for the team.

Terms & Conditions

1. By registering, teams agree to abide by all local stadium regulations and safety protocols.

2. The Pavilion Organising Committee reserves the right to reschedule matches due to inclement weather or unforeseen venue issues.

3. Registration fees are non-refundable once the tournament bracket has been finalized and published.

4. All participants must hold active medical insurance. The organizers are not liable for on-field injuries.

5. Teams must arrive at the venue at least 60 minutes prior to their scheduled start time.

7. Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited within the Ground A premises.
